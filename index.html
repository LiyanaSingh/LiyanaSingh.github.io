About Me

Hi! I'm a dedicated and passionate student currently pursuing my degree in Engineering. I’ve maintained an A-average throughout my academic journey, from primary school right through to university, thanks to my deep commitment to learning and personal growth.

While academics are important to me, I also embrace life outside the classroom. I’m a fun-loving person who enjoys spending quality time with friends and family. Whether it’s sharing a meal, going for a hike, swimming, heading out on a safari, or off-roading in my trusty Jimny, I’m always up for an adventure and creating lasting memories.

My relationships mean the world to me. I’m known among my friends as someone they can rely on, someone who shows up, listens, and cares deeply. My advice is often sought after, and I take pride in being the person who brings emotional insight and empathy into tough situations. I feel things deeply and often take on more than I should, but I wouldn’t have it any other way.

One of the defining aspects of my life has been my journey with arthritis. Diagnosed at just five years old, I faced a challenging treatment process early on but went into remission for many years. After contracting COVID-19 at the end of my matric year, my condition flared up again, and I began another round of intense treatment during my first year at university. Despite these challenges, I refused to let them define me or hold me back. I’ve continued to excel academically and make the most of my university experience and I’m happy to say that my arthritis is now fully under control again.

My story is one of resilience, determination, and balance between hard work and fun, strength and sensitivity, challenges and victories. I look forward to what lies ahead, both in my career and in life.
